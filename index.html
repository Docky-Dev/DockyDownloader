Yes, our service is 100% free with no hidden costs. There are no limits on the number of downloads, video length or quality. We offer this solution for free because we believe in an accessible internet for everyone.

What is the maximum available quality?

We support resolutions up to 4K (2160p) for videos when available on YouTube. For audio, we offer up to 320kbps in MP3, which provides excellent sound quality.

Is it legal to download YouTube videos?

Our service is a neutral technological tool. Legality depends on how you use it. We encourage you to only download content you own the rights to or that is under a free license. Check YouTube's terms of use for more information.

How can I download a full playlist?

Simply copy the YouTube playlist URL and paste it into our tool. You can then choose to download all items in the playlist at once or select specific videos.

My download is stuck, what should I do?

If you encounter issues, first try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, check that the URL is correct. For very long videos, processing may take a few minutes. If needed, our support is available 24/7.
